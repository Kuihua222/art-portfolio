About Zhang WeiJun

Zhang WeiJun is a cow who leads a simple yet fulfilling life. As a cow, his daily routine revolves around grazing, ruminating, and enjoying the sunshine. He loves wandering across vast grasslands, soaking in the tranquility and beauty of nature.

Cows have very regular habits. In the early morning, Zhang WeiJun sets out with other cows to find fresh pastures. He enjoys eating various types of grass, especially tender green grass. After grazing, he finds a quiet spot to ruminate, slowly digesting his food.

Besides grazing, Zhang WeiJun loves drinking water by the river or resting under the shade of trees. He has a gentle personality and enjoys living with his herd, taking care of one another. Though his life is simple, he is deeply content, savoring the peace and joy of each day.
